Who am I??

I envision myself as the Product Owner for Quality and the person who will interact with teams to ensure that we develop the capabilities necessary to improve the quality of a company. I am a firm believer in community building and like collaborating with internal and external teams to push quality as a shared objective while improving the skills of individuals. Metrics are an integral part of my work process. I appreciate creating the appropriate scorecards for each team in order to guarantee that data-driven choices are made and the appropriate competencies are developed. I am proactive and enjoy presenting the team's accomplishments to stakeholders as well as accepting accountability for our shortcomings. I am a firm believer of learning from your errors, and not repeating them.
